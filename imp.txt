<!-- FAQ Section -->
<div class="faq-section" id="faq">
  <h2 class="section-title">Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

  <!-- FAQ 1 -->
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h5 class="faq-question">What is the Olympics?</h5>
    <p class="faq-answer">The Olympics are a leading international sporting event featuring summer and winter sports competitions in which thousands of athletes participate in various games and events.</p>
  </div>

  <!-- FAQ 2 -->
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h5 class="faq-question">How often are the Olympic Games held?</h5>
    <p class="faq-answer">The Olympic Games are held every four years, alternating between Summer and Winter Olympics every two years.</p>
  </div>

  <!-- FAQ 3 -->
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h5 class="faq-question">Who can participate in the Olympics?</h5>
    <p class="faq-answer">Any athlete who meets the qualifications set by their sport's governing body and represents a recognized National Olympic Committee can participate in the Games.</p>
  </div>

  <!-- FAQ 4 -->
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h5 class="faq-question">What are the Olympic symbols?</h5>
    <p class="faq-answer">The Olympic symbols include the five interlocking rings, representing the unity of the five inhabited continents, the Olympic flame, and the official Olympic motto: "Citius, Altius, Fortius" (Faster, Higher, Stronger).</p>
  </div>

  <!-- FAQ 5 -->
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h5 class="faq-question">When was the first modern Olympics held?</h5>
    <p class="faq-answer">The first modern Olympic Games were held in Athens, Greece, in 1896.</p>
  </div>

  <!-- FAQ 6 -->
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h5 class="faq-question">How is the host city for the Olympics chosen?</h5>
    <p class="faq-answer">The host city is chosen by the International Olympic Committee (IOC) through a bidding process in which cities submit proposals and demonstrate their ability to host the Games.</p>
  </div>

  <!-- FAQ 7 -->
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h5 class="faq-question">What sports are included in the Olympics?</h5>
    <p class="faq-answer">The Olympics feature a wide variety of sports, ranging from athletics and swimming to newer sports like skateboarding and surfing.</p>
  </div>

  <!-- FAQ 8 -->
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h5 class="faq-question">What are the Paralympic Games?</h5>
    <p class="faq-answer">The Paralympic Games are a major international sports competition for athletes with disabilities, held immediately after the Olympic Games using the same venues.</p>
  </div>

  <!-- FAQ 9 -->
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h5 class="faq-question">How are athletes selected for the Olympics?</h5>
    <p class="faq-answer">Athletes are selected for the Olympics by their respective National Olympic Committees, based on their performance in qualifying competitions.</p>
  </div>

  <!-- FAQ 10 -->
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h5 class="faq-question">What is the Olympic motto?</h5>
    <p class="faq-answer">The Olympic motto is "Citius, Altius, Fortius," which is Latin for "Faster, Higher, Stronger."</p>
  </div>
